Welcome to PlayStation® – Play Has No Limits

Since our debut in 1994, PlayStation has been at the forefront of gaming innovation, transforming how the world plays, connects, and imagines. From the original PlayStation® console to the power of PlayStation®5, we've pushed the boundaries of entertainment to create experiences that inspire and unite players across the globe. At the heart of PlayStation is a passion for storytelling, creativity, and technology. Through iconic franchises like God of War™, The Last of Us™, Horizon™, and Spider-Man™, and with the support of world-class developers and studios, we deliver games that define generations and create lasting memories. As a brand of Sony Interactive Entertainment, PlayStation connects a global community of millions through immersive gameplay, cutting-edge hardware, and innovative services like PlayStation®Plus and PlayStation™Network. Whether you're playing solo or online with friends, on console or through the cloud, we’re committed to delivering the best in gaming. What We Stand For Innovation: We pioneer new experiences through technology and design. Community: We empower a diverse and inclusive global network of players and creators. Quality: We uphold the highest standards in everything we create—from consoles to games to services. Play: We believe in the power of play to inspire, challenge, and bring joy. Our Vision To shape the future of interactive entertainment and redefine what’s possible in gaming and beyond.
